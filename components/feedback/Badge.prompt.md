Circular certification badge for BIO, VEGAN and other quality marks. Appears on bottle labels and product cards.

```jsx
<Badge variant="bio">BIO</Badge>
<Badge variant="vegan">VEGAN</Badge>
<Badge variant="bio" size="sm">BIO<br/>VEGAN</Badge>
<Badge variant="gold" size="lg">2023</Badge>
```

Variants: `default`, `bio` (green outline), `vegan` (green outline), `organic` (green fill), `gold` (harvest gold).
Sizes: `sm` (36px), `md` (46px, default), `lg` (60px).
