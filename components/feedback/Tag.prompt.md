Pill-shaped attribute tag for terroir labels, wine categories and vintage years. Appears on product cards and filter bars.

```jsx
<Tag>LEHM</Tag>
<Tag>KALK</Tag>
<Tag variant="green">BIO</Tag>
<Tag variant="green">VEGAN</Tag>
<Tag variant="soil">2022</Tag>
<Tag variant="muted" size="xs">NATURWEIN</Tag>
```

Variants: `default` (warm border), `soil` (brown accent), `green` (vine leaf), `filled` (black fill), `muted` (subtle).
Sizes: `xs`, `sm` (default), `md`.
